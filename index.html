This is a RF linked Level sensing project

What is it:

This project uses an Ultrasonic transducer connected to an RF transmitter. I have used it to sens the Water level on my overhead tank. The receiver on the other end connected to a stepper motor to deflect a needle to show the continuous level sensing on a scale of High-Half-Low-Empty.
